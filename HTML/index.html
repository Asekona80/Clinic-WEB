About Us

Machubeni Clinic is proudly located in the village of Gxojeni, near Nonkqubela School. This clinic was built to serve the healthcare needs of rural communities in our area, ensuring quality medical services are accessible to all.

We provide compassionate and personalized healthcare with integrity and excellence. Our team works closely with patients to foster a supportive environment where everyone feels valued and cared for.

Our services range from general check-ups and immunizations to chronic disease management and maternal care — supporting you at every step of your healthcare journey.

Machubeni Clinic is more than a healthcare provider — we are your trusted partner in building a healthier future.

Our Services

At HealthCare Clinic, we are committed to providing compassionate, comprehensive, and accessible healthcare. Our wide range of services includes:

At Machubeni Clinic, we are proud to serve the people of our village and nearby rural areas with care and compassion. We provide general health check-ups to help you stay well and catch any health concerns early. If you need to see a specialist—such as a doctor for children, women’s health, or skin conditions—we are here for you. In case of emergencies or sudden illness, our team is ready to give quick and reliable help.

You can collect your prescribed medicines conveniently from our clinic pharmacy. We offer vaccinations for children and adults, including flu shots and COVID-19 vaccines. For mothers-to-be, we provide prenatal care and support during pregnancy and childbirth.

We also help manage chronic conditions like diabetes, high blood pressure, and asthma with regular check-ups and advice. Our mental health support services offer a listening ear and counseling for individuals and families. We conduct basic lab tests right at the clinic, saving you the need to travel far. And because we believe in healthy living, we run community health talks to share knowledge and support a better lifestyle for everyone.

Our Mission

At Machubeni Clinic, we are dedicated to improving the health and well-being of our rural community. We strive to provide accessible, affordable, and high-quality healthcare with compassion and respect for every individual we serve.

Our commitment is to deliver excellent care that meets the unique needs of people living in rural areas, ensuring they receive the medical attention they deserve regardless of their background or financial situation.

We believe in proactive wellness, focusing not only on treating illness but also on prevention through health education, early detection, and community outreach programs. Our goal is to empower every person in our community with the knowledge and resources to take control of their health.

Supported by a team of passionate healthcare professionals, we maintain the highest standards of care while fostering a supportive and welcoming environment for all our patients.
